Sample Email to Student 2:

Email Title: {{{Recipient.FirstName}}}, Academic Assistance for Your Success at Hillsborough Community College

Dear {{{Recipient.FirstName}}},

We hope this email finds you well. On behalf of Hillsborough Community College, we wanted to reach out to discuss your current academic status and offer support to help you improve your GPA. We understand that everyone faces challenges from time to time, and we're here to help you overcome them.

First and foremost, please remember that you're not alone. Many students experience academic setbacks, and we have resources available to assist you in getting back on track.

Here are a few supportive measures you can consider:
  • Academic Advising: Our dedicated advisors are here to guide you through this situation. Schedule a meeting to discuss your academic goals, review your course load, and develop a plan to improve your performance.
  • Academic Success Centers: Our tutoring center offers one-on-one support to help you better understand challenging concepts and enhance your study skills. Take advantage of this valuable resource to excel in your coursework.
  • TRIO Resources: Collaborating with peers can greatly benefit your learning experience. Consider joining a study group to share knowledge, exchange ideas, and reinforce your understanding of the material.

    • Remember, we believe in your potential to overcome this setback and thrive academically. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or need further assistance. Together, we can work towards improving your GPA and achieving your goals.

    Best regards,

    Hillsborough Community College
