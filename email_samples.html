By taking proactive steps and seeking the help available to you, we are confident that you can get back on track and achieve your academic goals. If you have any questions or require further information, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are here to support you throughout your journey at Hillsborough Community College.

Best regards,

The Hillsborough Community College Team
