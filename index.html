Hillsborough Community College Early Alert System

This system is designed to support the success of students by identifying potential challenges early on and providing timely interventions to ensure their academic progress and well-being.

How It Works

The Early Alert System utilizes the robust capabilities of HCC's CRM, Salesforce, and integrates with their ERP, Ellucian Colleague, to collect and analyze student data. Through a combination of automated processes and faculty input, the system identifies students who may be facing academic difficulties, attendance issues, or other challenges that may hinder their progress.

Strategy

The goal would be to establish an effective communications loop between "front line" offices, students, and the early alert team.

    Features and Benefits

    Early Identification: The system allows for the early identification of students who may require additional support. By flagging specific indicators such as low grades, attendance patterns, or lack of engagement, faculty and advisors can intervene promptly.

    Collaborative Approach: The Early Alert System fosters collaboration among faculty, advisors, and support staff. It provides a centralized platform where relevant stakeholders can access student information, exchange notes, and coordinate efforts to address student needs effectively.

    Timely Interventions: With automated alerts and notifications, the system ensures that faculty and advisors are promptly informed about student challenges. This enables them to provide timely interventions, such as one-on-one advising, tutoring, or referrals to support services, to assist students in overcoming obstacles.

    Data-Driven Decision Making: By leveraging the data collected in Salesforce and Ellucian Colleague, the Early Alert System enables administrators and faculty to gain insights into student performance trends, identify systemic issues, and implement evidence-based strategies to improve student success rates.

    Enhanced Communication: The system facilitates improved communication between faculty, advisors, and students. Through the system, students receive personalized messages, recommendations, and resources to support their academic journey.

    Progress Tracking: The Early Alert System enables ongoing monitoring of student progress. Faculty and advisors can document interventions, track outcomes, and assess the effectiveness of support strategies implemented, ensuring continuous improvement in student success initiatives.
